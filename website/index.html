About

Mysterious, charismatic and brilliant professor Annalise Keating offers the course `How to Get Away with Murder' at Middleton Law School. She also works as a criminal defense attorney. Annalise chooses five particularly ambitious and highly intelligent students from her class to help her defend her clients. Whoever does the best job and particularly impresses Annalise is to be rewarded with a trophy. Students Wes, Michaela, Laurel, Connor and Asher are very ambitious, each wanting to win. But soon they come across dark secrets and are pushed to their limits - and beyond - in solving the cases.

Season 1

How to Get Away With Murder - Season 1 revolves around professor and criminal defense attorney Annalise Keating, who becomes involved in various murder cases along with five of her students.

Season 2

Season 2 focuses on the case of two siblings and their involvement in the murder of their adoptive parents. Wes also goes in search of the missing Rebecca.

Season 3

In season 3, an unknown man tries to ruin Annelise's reputation at the university by distributing flyers denouncing her as a murderer. Eventually, Annelise even ends up in the county jail after being blamed for a murder.

Season 4

In the 15 episodes of Season 4, the Keating 4 try to uncover the true background to Wes' murder, setting in motion a chain of brutal events.
